OurSpot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

Overview

OurSpot ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address - Used for account authentication and important notifications
  • Display name - Shown to other users on your profile and events
  • Profile photo - Optional, displayed on your profile

Location Data

  • Device location - Used to show nearby events on the map (only when you grant permission)
  • Event locations - Addresses you enter when creating events

User-Generated Content

  • Events you create (title, description, date, location)
  • Chat messages in event group chats
  • RSVP responses to events

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To show you relevant events near your location
  • To enable communication between event attendees
  • To send push notifications about events and messages (if enabled)
  • To improve and optimize the app experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared in these limited circumstances:

  • With other users - Your profile information and events are visible to other app users
  • Service providers - We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication
  • Legal requirements - If required by law or to protect safety

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through Supabase
  • Row-level security policies on all database tables

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View your personal data in your profile
  • Correct - Update your profile information at any time
  • Delete - Delete your account and associated data from Settings
  • Opt-out - Disable push notifications in your device settings

Children's Privacy

OurSpot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.
